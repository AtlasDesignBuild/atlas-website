Local knowledge

Remodeling kitchens in Yardley and Lower Makefield

Many of the most sought-after homes in Yardley Borough and Lower Makefield Township were built between the 1960s and 1990s. These are well-built homes with good bones, but their kitchens often reflect the era — closed floor plans, dated cabinetry and layouts that do not work the way families live today. Opening the kitchen to the living or dining space, reconfiguring the layout and upgrading the finishes consistently transforms how a home feels.

Yardley Borough has its own permitting process separate from Lower Makefield Township. We handle permitting on every project and factor the local timeline into the schedule from the start. For homes near the Delaware Canal or within flood-zone adjacency, we are familiar with the additional considerations that can affect a remodel plan.
