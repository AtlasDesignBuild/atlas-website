Local knowledge

Remodeling kitchens in Newtown Borough and Newtown Township

Newtown Borough homes tend to be older and more architecturally distinctive — Federal, Greek Revival and Victorian styles are common along the main streets. These kitchens often have smaller footprints and original layouts that were designed for a different era. Thoughtful reconfiguration, custom cabinetry built to fit non-standard dimensions and carefully chosen finishes are what make these projects succeed.

Newtown Township homes skew newer and larger, with more open floor plans that benefit from elevated finishes, custom cabinetry and design-forward details rather than structural changes. We handle permitting for both municipalities and factor the local timeline into every project schedule from the start.
