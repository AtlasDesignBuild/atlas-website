Basement finishing in Bucks County, PA

Atlas Design & Build is a husband and wife led design build firm finishing and remodeling basements across Bucks County and Montgomery County, Pennsylvania. What our basement projects include

Each basement renovation is built around how you actually want to use the space. Common scope elements include:

  • Full layout design including family rooms, home theaters, guest suites, gyms and offices
  • Framing, drywall, ceiling treatments, paint and trim
  • Moisture management and waterproofing
  • Code compliant egress windows and stairways
  • Electrical, lighting design and HVAC extension
  • Plumbing for basement bathrooms, wet bars and kitchenettes
  • Flooring in luxury vinyl, tile, carpet and engineered hardwood

Permits and inspections are handled by us. If your project needs structural changes or whole home considerations, we plan and execute those alongside the basement work.

Questions

Frequently asked questions

How much does it cost to finish a basement in Bucks County?

Basement finishing costs in Bucks County depend on scope, layout, plumbing additions and the condition of the existing space. A basic finished family room can start in the high four to low five figures. Larger projects with bathrooms, wet bars, custom built ins and theaters commonly run into the mid to high five figures, sometimes into the low six figures for the largest scopes. We share a realistic budget range during the discovery conversation, before any design work begins. Read our full guide to basement finishing costs in Bucks County.

How long does it take to finish a basement?

Most Atlas basement projects run six to ten weeks of active construction once design and permits are complete. The full timeline from first conversation to final walkthrough generally lands between three and five months. Adding bathrooms, wet bars or significant structural work extends both ranges. We build the schedule with you upfront and share it in writing.

Do you handle moisture and waterproofing?

Yes. Moisture management is the single most important factor in whether a finished basement holds up over time. Every project begins with an assessment of the existing waterproofing, drainage and humidity. We address any moisture issues before framing and finishing begin, not after.

Do you handle the design as well as the construction?

Yes. As a design build firm, design and construction are handled under one roof. You work with a single team from layout planning through final installation. This keeps decisions consistent and avoids the gaps that appear between separate design and construction firms.
