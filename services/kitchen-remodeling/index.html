How we work

Our process

Every Atlas kitchen project runs through the same structured process. You always know what is happening and what is next.

01

Discovery

We meet at your home, walk the space and talk through how you want to use the kitchen. Realistic budget ranges are on the table before any design work begins.

02

Design and planning

Layouts, materials, finishes and lighting are detailed before construction starts. Final pricing, scope, scheduling and permits are confirmed in writing.

03

Construction

One of us is in the field every day. Job sites are kept clean and respected. Progress updates are shared regularly so you are never wondering what happens next.

04

Final walkthrough

Every detail is reviewed with you in person. Anything that does not meet our standard or yours is corrected before the project closes.

What our kitchen remodels include

Each kitchen renovation is built around how you actually live in your home. Common scope elements include:

  • Full layout design and reconfiguration
  • Custom and semi custom cabinetry
  • Countertops, backsplash, tile and stone work
  • Flooring in hardwood, tile and luxury vinyl
  • Plumbing, electrical and lighting design
  • Custom metal and glass detail work, one of our specialties
  • Appliance integration, built ins, paint and trim

Permits and inspections are handled by us. If your project needs structural changes or whole home considerations, we plan and execute those alongside the kitchen work.

Questions

Frequently asked questions

How much does a kitchen remodel cost in Bucks County?

Kitchen remodel costs depend on scope, finishes, layout changes and the condition of the existing space. Smaller updates can start in the mid five figures. Full renovations with custom cabinetry and layout changes commonly run into the six figures. We share a realistic budget range during the discovery conversation, before any design work begins. Read our full guide to kitchen remodel costs in Bucks County.

How long does a kitchen remodel take?

Most Atlas kitchen renovations run six to twelve weeks of active construction once design and permits are complete. The full timeline from first conversation to final walkthrough generally lands between three and five months. We build the schedule with you upfront and share it in writing.

What is included in your kitchen remodels?

Each project is scoped individually. Most include design, cabinetry, countertops, backsplash, flooring, lighting, plumbing, electrical, paint and trim. Custom metal and glass, structural changes and integrated appliance work are available. Permits and inspections are handled by us.

Do you handle the design as well as the construction?

Yes. As a design build firm, design and construction are handled under one roof. You work with a single team from layout planning through final installation. This keeps decisions consistent and avoids the gaps that appear between separate design and construction firms.
