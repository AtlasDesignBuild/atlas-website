Local knowledge

Remodeling bathrooms in Langhorne and Middletown Township

Many homes in the Langhorne area were built in the postwar decades, particularly the 1950s through the 1970s. Bathrooms from this era are often small, compartmentalized and finished with materials that have not aged well. The most common starting point for homeowners in this area is a primary bathroom that no longer meets the needs of how they live: undersized, poorly lit and finished in ways that feel dated.

We work within Langhorne Borough, Langhorne Manor and Middletown Township and understand how each municipality handles permitting. We pull permits on every project and coordinate the inspection schedule so the work stays on track from start to finish.
