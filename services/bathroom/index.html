Our scope

What our bathroom remodeling services include

Every bathroom we build starts with a conversation about how you actually use the space. From there we handle design, permitting, construction and finish selection, start to finish.

Full layout reconfiguration

Primary, secondary and powder room rebuilds including plumbing and structural changes handled with permits in place.

Custom and semi-custom vanities

Built to the exact dimensions and storage needs of your bathroom, not adapted from a standard box.

Tile, stone and mosaic work

Custom shower surrounds, full height stone walls, floor patterns and mosaic details selected and installed as part of the overall design.

Frameless glass and custom metal work

Custom glass enclosures and metal details fabricated in-house. This is an Atlas specialty that sets our bathrooms apart.

Plumbing, electrical and lighting

Vanity, ambient and task lighting designed as part of the overall plan. Plumbing and electrical handled by licensed trades we work with on every project.

Heated flooring and waterproofing

Radiant heat, proper waterproofing membranes and ventilation specified and installed correctly from the start.

Steam showers, soaking tubs and wet rooms

High end fixtures and specialty features integrated cleanly into the design and the build.

Permitting and inspections, handled by us

We pull permits, coordinate inspections and keep the project on schedule throughout.

Bathrooms are smaller than kitchens but every bit as complex. Tile, waterproofing, custom glass, ventilation, plumbing, lighting and finish work all converge in a few hundred square feet. The room only feels right when every detail lands. We carry the weight of planning, executing and managing each of those moving parts so you do not have to.

Questions

Frequently asked questions

How much does a bathroom remodel cost in Bucks County?

Most Bucks County bathroom remodels run between $25,000 and $95,000 depending on scope and finish level. Half baths and powder rooms typically fall in the $25,000 to $50,000 range. Primary bathrooms run $45,000 to $95,000. Primary suites with custom features start at $110,000. Final pricing depends on scope, finishes, layout changes and the condition of the existing space.

How long does a bathroom remodel take?

Most Atlas bathroom renovations run four to eight weeks of active construction once design and permits are complete. The full timeline from first conversation to final walkthrough generally lands between two and four months. We build the schedule with you upfront and share it in writing.

Can you remodel a small bathroom?

Yes. Small bathrooms are some of the most rewarding projects to design. Layout reconfiguration, custom storage, frameless glass, the right tile scale and intentional lighting can transform a powder room or compact full bath into something that feels larger, more functional and more refined.

Do you handle design as well as construction?

Yes. As a design build firm, design and construction are handled under one roof. You work with a single team from layout planning through final installation. This keeps decisions consistent and avoids the gaps that appear between separate design and construction firms.
