Bathroom remodeling

Bathroom remodeling in Bucks County, PA

Atlas Design & Build is a husband and wife led design build firm renovating bathrooms across Bucks County and Montgomery County, Pennsylvania. From small powder room refreshes through full primary bathroom rebuilds, we specialize in renovations that combine elevated design with quality craftsmanship and an organized process from start to finish.

Bathrooms are smaller than kitchens but every bit as complex. Tile, waterproofing, custom glass, ventilation, plumbing, lighting and finish work all converge in a few hundred square feet. The room only feels right when every detail lands. We carry the weight of planning, executing and managing each of those moving parts so you do not have to.

How we work

Our process

Every Atlas bathroom project runs through the same structured process. You always know what is happening and what is next.

01

Discovery

We meet at your home, walk the space and talk through how you want to use the bathroom. Realistic budget ranges are on the table before any design work begins.

02

Design and planning

Layouts, materials, finishes and lighting are detailed before construction starts. Final pricing, scope, scheduling and permits are confirmed in writing.

03

Construction

One of us is in the field every day. Job sites are kept clean and respected. Progress updates are shared regularly so you are never wondering what happens next.

04

Final walkthrough

Every detail is reviewed with you in person. Anything that does not meet our standard or yours is corrected before the project closes.

What our bathroom remodels include

Each bathroom renovation is built around how you actually live in your home. Common scope elements include:

  • Full layout reconfiguration including primary, secondary and powder room rebuilds
  • Custom and semi custom vanities
  • Tile, stone and mosaic work including custom shower surrounds
  • Tubs, showers and frameless glass enclosures, custom metal and glass work is an Atlas specialty
  • Plumbing, electrical and lighting design including vanity, ambient and task lighting
  • Heated flooring, ventilation and waterproofing
  • Storage solutions, paint and trim

Permits and inspections are handled by us. If your project needs structural changes or whole home considerations, we plan and execute those alongside the bathroom work.

Questions

Frequently asked questions

How much does a bathroom remodel cost in Bucks County?

Most Bucks County bathroom remodels run in the low to mid five figures for moderate scope and into the low six figures for full primary bathroom renovations with custom features. Powder rooms and smaller secondary bathrooms can come in lower. Final pricing depends on scope, finishes, layout changes and the condition of the existing space. We share a realistic budget range during the discovery conversation, before any design work begins. Read our full guide to bathroom remodel costs in Bucks County.

How long does a bathroom remodel take?

Most Atlas bathroom renovations run four to eight weeks of active construction once design and permits are complete. The full timeline from first conversation to final walkthrough generally lands between two and four months. We build the schedule with you upfront and share it in writing.

Can you remodel a small bathroom?

Yes. Small bathrooms are some of the most rewarding projects to design. Layout reconfiguration, custom storage, frameless glass, the right tile scale and intentional lighting can transform a powder room or compact full bath into something that feels larger, more functional and more refined.

Do you handle the design as well as the construction?

Yes. As a design build firm, design and construction are handled under one roof. You work with a single team from layout planning through final installation. This keeps decisions consistent and avoids the gaps that appear between separate design and construction firms.
