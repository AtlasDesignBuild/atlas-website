Whole home remodeling

Whole home remodeling in Bucks County, PA

Atlas Design & Build is a husband and wife led design build firm leading whole home renovations across Bucks County and Montgomery County, Pennsylvania. We work with homeowners opening up floor plans they have outgrown, modernizing older homes without losing their character and rebuilding tired layouts into homes that feel current.

A whole home renovation has more moving parts than any other residential project. Multiple rooms, multiple trades, structural and systems decisions, often a family living in part of the house while construction continues. We carry the weight of planning, coordinating and communicating across every part of the project so you do not have to track it yourself.

What our whole home renovations include

Each whole home renovation is built around how you actually want to live in the house. Common scope elements include:

  • Multi room design and phased layout planning
  • Kitchen and bathroom renovations
  • Basement finishing and primary suite reconfigurations
  • Living, dining, family room, mudroom and laundry renovations
  • Structural changes including wall removals and small additions
  • Electrical, plumbing and HVAC updates across the home
  • Flooring, paint, trim, lighting and finish work throughout

Permits and inspections are handled by us.

Questions

Frequently asked questions

How much does a whole home renovation cost in Bucks County?

Whole home renovation costs in Bucks County and Montgomery County vary widely based on scope, finishes, structural changes and the condition of the existing home. Multi room interior renovations typically start in the low six figures. Larger whole home projects with full kitchen, multiple bathrooms, basement finishing and significant structural work commonly run into the mid to high six figures. We share a realistic budget range during the discovery conversation, before any design work begins. Explore our whole home renovation cost guide.

How long does a whole home renovation take?

Most Atlas whole home renovations run four to nine months of active construction once design and permits are complete. The full timeline from first conversation to final walkthrough commonly lands between six and twelve months. Phased renovations completed in stages can take longer overall but reduce the impact on family life during construction. We build the schedule with you upfront and share it in writing.

Can we live in the house during a whole home renovation?

In many cases yes, especially when the project is phased or limited to specific rooms at a time. Full gut renovations and significant structural changes generally require temporarily relocating. We help you think through what is realistic for your project during the discovery conversation, and plan the construction sequence around your family's needs where possible.

Do you handle the design as well as the construction?

Yes. As a design build firm, design and construction are handled under one roof. You work with a single team from layout planning through final installation. This keeps decisions consistent and avoids the gaps that appear between separate design and construction firms.
