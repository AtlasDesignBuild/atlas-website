How much does a bathroom remodel cost in Bucks County, PA?

By Atlas Design & Build · Bathroom remodeling

Most bathroom remodels in Bucks County run from the low to mid five figures for a moderate renovation into the low six figures for a full primary bathroom with custom features. Powder rooms and smaller secondary bathrooms generally come in lower.

Bathrooms are smaller than kitchens but every bit as complex. Tile, waterproofing, custom glass, ventilation, plumbing, lighting and finish work all converge in a few hundred square feet, and the room only feels right when every detail lands. The guide below explains what bathroom renovations cost in Bucks County, what moves the number and how to plan a budget that holds.

What drives a bathroom remodel up or down

A few hundred square feet hold a surprising amount of decisions. These are the factors that move the cost the most:

  • Layout changes. Keeping the toilet, shower and vanity in place holds costs down. Moving plumbing adds time and expense.
  • Tile and stone. The amount of tile, the material and the complexity of the pattern all affect both labor and material cost.
  • Showers and glass. A frameless glass enclosure, a custom shower pan and a built in bench raise the scope. Custom metal and glass work is an Atlas specialty handled in house.
  • Fixtures and finishes. Faucets, lighting, vanities and hardware span a wide range from standard to high end.
  • Waterproofing and ventilation. Done correctly, these are what keep a bathroom sound over time. We treat them as essential, not optional.
  • The condition of the existing space. Older homes can hide water damage or dated plumbing behind the walls. We plan for that upfront.

Small bathrooms are some of the most rewarding projects to design. Layout reconfiguration, custom storage, frameless glass, the right tile scale and intentional lighting can transform a compact space into something that feels larger and more refined.

How long does a bathroom remodel take?

Most bathroom renovations run four to eight weeks of active construction once design and permits are complete. The full timeline from first conversation to final walkthrough generally lands between two and four months. We build the schedule with you upfront and share it in writing.

How to budget with confidence

The cleanest way to avoid surprises is to define the project in detail before any work begins. After the first conversation you receive a written proposal outlining the full scope of work, materials, timeline and pricing. We refine it together so you have a clear picture of what is included and what is not before construction starts.

The takeaway

A bathroom remodel in Bucks County can be a focused update or a full custom rebuild, and the range reflects that. Layout, tile, glass and finishes are the levers. The right partner helps you weigh those choices early, prices the work honestly and executes the details that make a bathroom feel effortless to use.

Frequently asked questions

How much does it cost to remodel a small bathroom in Bucks County?

Powder rooms and compact secondary bathrooms generally start in the low five figures. The final figure depends on the finishes you choose and whether the layout and plumbing stay in place.

Can you remodel a bathroom in an older home?

Yes. We have decades of experience with older Bucks County homes, including Victorian rowhomes, farmhouses and historic properties. We assess the existing plumbing, structure and waterproofing before we begin so the surprises common in older homes are planned for rather than discovered mid project.
